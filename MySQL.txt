An organization maintains employment data in three tables: EMPLOYEE,COMPANY, and SALARY. Write a query to print the names of every company where the average salary is greater than or equal to 40000 Each distinct row of results in the output must contain the name of a company whose average employee salary is â‰¥ 40,000 in the COMPANY.NAME format.

select distinct(a.NAME) from COMPANY as a 
inner join SALARY as b on a.ID = b.COMPANY_ID 
GROUP by a.NAME having avg(b.SALARY) >=40000;
